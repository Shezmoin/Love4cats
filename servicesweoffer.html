Services we offer


Promoting cat safety

Promoting cat safety involves creating awareness about potential hazards in the environment and encouraging responsible pet ownership. By advocating for cat safety measures such as identification tags, microchipping, and safe outdoor spaces, we strive to ensure a secure and nurturing environment for our feline companions.



Catsafe Education

Educating on cat safety involves teaching pet owners about potential dangers, proper nutrition, and providing a secure environment for their feline friends. Through educational initiatives, we aim to empower cat owners with the knowledge and skills needed to enhance the safety and well-being of their beloved cats.



Understanding Behaviour

Discover the intricacies of your cat's communication by exploring our comprehensive guides on feline behavior, covering topics such as interpreting body language, recognizing signs of stress, understanding reasons behind hiding, and addressing spraying behaviors.

Grief and loss

In times of sorrow, whether coping with the loss of your cat, facing challenging decisions like euthanasia, dealing with a missing pet, or seeking emotional support, we are here to provide understanding and assistance. Recognizing the deep bond between you and your cat, we offer a range of resources and support.



Cat Guardians

Our Cat Guardians service ensures continuous care for your cat with a dedicated cuddle-giver, behind-the-ear-scratcher, and food provider, even in the event of your passing. Ensure your feline friend's happiness by planning for their well-being, considering what would happen to them if something were to happen to you.



Fostering Lifeline

Providing a confidential and complimentary fostering service, we ensure the well-being of cats from families escaping domestic abuse, offering assurance that their feline companions will be cared for until a safe reunion is possible.
